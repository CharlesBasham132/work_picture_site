About Speak Easy

  • Speak Easy is an online application which allows you to find your voice. Designed specifically for elderly / disabled persons whom may have difficulty with speech or language barriers.
  • Communicating with people, be it carers or family members, the Speak Easy website is here to provide a simple method of communication which is easy and intuative to use.
  • Instead of strugling or having to point at items, simply click the desired button and the application will speak aloud. For example instead of writing a note to say you are hungry, just click the 'hunger' button from the Home Screen.
